About Us

VHD Finance & Resolution Agency, founded by Mr. Devendra Salve in Khandwa, MP, is committed to helping Indian banks with trusted loan recovery and resolution support. With deep regional knowledge and a commitment to transparency, we ensure timely results and reliable field presence.
